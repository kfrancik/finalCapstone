Kate's Corner

Interested in handmade jewellery? Come here!

My name is Kate and I love handmade jewellery. Why? Because every piece of jewellery is special. Because I love to design it myself. Kate's Corner is a place where I want to share my passion with others. What charms me, inspires me and what I create. I invite you to my world of creation.

My Jewellery

I have always loved original jewellery. Why? Because jewellery helps me express my temperament and mood. There is a story behind every piece of my jewellery. People have always believed that jewellery has magical powers. It not only decorates but also protects against evil. You can believe it or not, but what is certain is that jewellery turns boring outfits into exciting ones.
My jewellery is mainly inspired by nature, which is an inexhaustible source of shapes, colours, and ideas. I make it in the technique of bead and soutache embroidery.

Projects

The soutache and bead embroidery techniques are used not only to create jewellery. Originally, these techniques were primarily used to decorate clothes. Currently, they are still sometimes used for this purpose. I am part of a project in which fairy-tale costumes are created. Creations of nearly a hundred soutache artists make up the final effect that can be admired at museum exhibitions and photo sessions.
